Lecturer in Bioinformatics for the MBIOINDI-MBA Master’s Program in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence for Pharma, Biotech, Healthcare, and MedTech industries. Delivered specialized training in bioinformatics fundamentals, sequence alignment methodologies (local, global, and glocal alignment), heuristic search algorithms (FASTA and BLAST), multiple sequence alignment approaches (Clustal, MUSCLE, T-Coffee), graph theory applications, and phylogenetic tree construction methods. Teaching also covered introductory computational concepts including algorithmic complexity, pseudocode, and practical programming foundations in Bash and Python applied to sequence analysis and computational biology workflows.
